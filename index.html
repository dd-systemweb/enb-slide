ENB Trade-In Process

Guidelines & Procedures Present By Danial

Main Process Steps

Approach Customer

Initiate contact and establish rapport with the customer

Give Estimated Price

Provide initial valuation based on device information

Do Diagnostic

Perform thorough device diagnostic to customer

Payment Plan Guidelines

CANNOT ACCEPT

Customer still under telco instalment

WE CAN ACCEPT

Customer purchased under Credit Card

Front Screen Photo Protocol

Required

Take photo for front screen check

Avoid

Taking from IMEI/notes apps/about setting/google

Reason

May affect price while checking

Solution

Take the front screen photo from QR by system generated

Internal & Physical Check (New Features)

Charging Port

MDM (Management Device)

Network

Carrier Lock

Sim Card Detection

If clicked YES for any of these features, automatically will be rejected

Additional Checks

Repair History Check

Examine device repair records to identify previous issues

Battery Replacement Check

Verify if battery has been replaced or is original

Bloated battery can be accepted

Customer Service Protocol

AVOID

Asking questions on WhatsApp group

INSTEAD

Find ENB Customer Service team to assist for any issues
